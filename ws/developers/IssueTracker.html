Issue Tracker Policy For Developers of petstore Project

We use the issue tracker of the petstore at https://petstore.dev.java.net/servlets/ProjectIssues

Under the petstore component, there are 3 sub-components
  1. General,  for most issues including RFEs
  2. download bundle, for the releases available to public on download section of project
  3. www for the online live docs  published petstore website

Creating a new Issue

Generally, when you create a new issue, just choose petstore component and then choose one of the subcomponent categories. In the drop down list for "Issue Type" there are choices such as  "Enhancement" for an RFE etc. That is all fairly obvious if you go to the issuetracker.
Lastly, and *importantly* when you fill in the "Summary" text please preface the summary with something descriptive so that it makes it easier to read the reports which just list the summaries, please.

In terms of RFEs, lets use the internal wiki to collect candidate use cases and components. Otherwise we end up with too many scrathpad ideas added in issuetracker. For enhancements/ RFEs of existing entries
